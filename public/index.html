Faqs

What is Tailoring?

Tailoring is an art by which a garment is altered according to an individual body shape. From the sleeve and shoulder-length to the inseam of a pair of denim jeans we tailor any garment.

Do I need to give my measurements during every visit?

Yes, for the best fit either a perfectly fitting garment or measurements has to be given during the visit.

What is the minimum charge for alteration?

The minimum charge is Rs 30/- for single garment and it also depends on the kind of alteration to be done.

What are the payment options available?

You can Pay us through cash, or through any of the UPI payment apps (Google Pay, BHIM, PhonePe).

Do I need to give my measurements every time I order?

Although a self-measurement can be done, we believe that the measurement is a critical aspect in getting the best fit so we have made it compulsory for our customers to get measured by one of the concierge stylists
